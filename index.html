PROBE-FDAA User Guide

How to use this tool

FDAAs have differential incorporation in different bacteria. Using this fact, we performed a screen of 7 compounds in 17 bacterial species to identify potential patterns of incorporation. You may use the results of this screen in this tool to perform cellular identification.

Select two species to compare. The system will determine which FDAAs may be used to distinguish them. Each number represents relative Mean Fluorescence Intensity (MFI) from flow cytometry. Rows highlighted in red indicate compounds predicted to distinguish the species. Growth times are also provided to guide incubation.

For questions, contact tcwarner@mit.edu.
